About This Territory Sales Officer Role in Navi Mumbai

Unilever, a global leader in consumer goods, is seeking a Territory Sales Officer in Navi Mumbai. As a Territory Sales Officer, you will be responsible for driving sales targets through personal selling and managing the distribution system effectively. Your role will involve analyzing market situations, managing distributor systems, and leveraging relationships with stakeholders to strengthen Unilever's market position.

Key Responsibilities

  • Achieve sales targets through market visits and distribution management.
  • Maintain and develop distributor systems, addressing both front-end and back-end needs.
  • Utilize MIS reports for identifying gaps and improving execution of sales strategies.
  • Negotiate with distributors, fostering staff development to enhance customer management.
  • Collect and utilize market feedback to enhance Unilever's strategies.
  • Enhance customer service to improve competitive market positioning.

Required Skills for Territory Sales Officer

  • Fulltime graduation required.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in Channel Sales.
  • Proficiency in ROI calculations and RS distribution management.
  • Experience in RS appointment and sales planning.
  • Competent in promotional activities and execution.
  • Knowledgeable in MS-Office, especially Excel.
  • Effective communication skills in English and local languages.

Company History

Founded in 1929, Unilever started as a soap manufacturer and has grown to become one of the world’s largest FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) companies. With products marketed under over 400 brands, Unilever serves millions of consumers every day globally.

Unilever has always prioritized sustainability and social responsibility, focusing on reducing its environmental footprint and promoting fair trade practices. Today, its mission includes making sustainable living commonplace.
